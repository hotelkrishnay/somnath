About Hotel Krishnay

Discover the story behind Hotel Krishnay, your budget-friendly retreat near Somnath Temple, offering comfort, hospitality, and a memorable stay.

Our Story

Who We Are

Hotel Krishnay, established in 2018, is a family-run budget hotel near Somnath Temple. We offer cozy, affordable stays with a warm, welcoming vibe, blending traditional hospitality with modern comfort to make every guest feel at home.

Our Mission

At Hotel Krishnay, we aim to provide clean, comfortable, and budget-friendly accommodation. Our warm hospitality and personalized service ensure guests feel at home, creating a serene retreat for pilgrims and travelers alike.

Why Choose Us

Located near Somnath Temple and Veraval Port, Hotel Krishnay offers unmatched convenience. Enjoy excellent facilities at affordable rates in a safe, secure environment, making us the ideal choice for a value-packed stay.

Facilities & Comfort

Our neat, spacious rooms come with essential amenities like free Wi-Fi, 24-hour front desk, and parking. We arrange delicious meals from nearby restaurants, ensuring a comfortable and hassle-free experience for all guests.

Who We Serve

Hotel Krishnay welcomes families, solo travelers, business guests, and tourists exploring Somnath, Diu, Keshod, Rajkot, and Porbandar. Our versatile accommodations cater to diverse needs, ensuring a delightful stay for all.

Our Promise

We promise friendly, attentive staff, impeccable cleanliness, and a focus on guest satisfaction. At Hotel Krishnay, we strive to create memorable stays, ensuring every guest leaves with cherished memories of their time with us.
